What Is It?

Site Feedback is an alternative option to ForeSee Feedback for clients who want to deploy a comment-card style survey on select/multiple pages within a single domain who can not deploy ACS-Hosted code solution. The survey is deployed through a static URL provided to the client who can make that URL available on any pages within a single domain and mask it with a vanity URL/Button or other call to action.

Available in these channels:

  • Web
  • Mobile (Site and App)

Why Use It?

Site Feedback allows companies to get immediate feedback from a visitor, providing a quick way to tell you what is on their mind at the particular moment. With Site Feedback, users do not have to wait until the survey invitation shows up, if it even shows up at all. If they are experiencing a technical issue or just want to share their opinion on the content or layout, they can initiate the survey and leave their feedback.

General Information

Typical Survey Design

  • Three Satisfaction questions (OSAT, Meets Expectations, Ideal Website) - Closed-end rating questions.
  • Topic of Feedback (Website/Technical issue, Product Comment, General Comment) - Closed-end, select one type question.
  • Type of Feedback (Issue, Suggestion, Comment, Praise) - Closed-end, select one type question.
  • Feedback - Open-end comment about feedback topic.

General Information

  • Alerts can be created if a specific rating or answer option is submitted.
  • The static link can be placed anywhere on the site, though it is typically place in the â€˜Contact Usâ€™ section.
  • Full data can be viewed in AAP, partial data access in EP (open-ends and trends).
  • Site Feedback allows for unlimited surveys within a single site/domain.
  • There is no persistent cookie to stop people from taking the survey multiple times.
  • Data can be exported into Excel or PDF format.
  • Site Feedback supports all the same languages as our standard CX surveys.
  • Smart Thank You Pages and Integration are possible with Site Feedback, though are seldom, if ever, used.
