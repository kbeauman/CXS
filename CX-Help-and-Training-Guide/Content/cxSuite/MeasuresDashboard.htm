Measures Dashboard

This dashboard is designed for detailed analysis of scores associated to a specific measure. You can access the Measure Dashboard by clicking on the desired measure on the CX 360 Dashboard, or by clicking Surveys in the Top Navigation Bar and selecting the measure from the menu.

Below the Top Navigation Bar is a secondary toolbar containing a menu of measures, a date picker, a menu of filters, and export commands. You can use this secondary toolbar to navigate to all your surveys, segment the data based on date intervals or filtered groups (like first time visitors), and you can export the displayed data into a PowerPoint presentation for disseminating information throughout your organization.

 

 

This dashboard displays a handful of default cards that tell you a.) How am I doing, How do I compare to myself and is the change significant or not, how do I compare to others (Gauge), b.) How am I performing over time (Trendview), c.) What do I need to fix and why does it matter (Score Summary Card), d.) Am I making the right improvements to the most impactful aspects of the customer experience (Priority map) and e.) What are customers saying about their experience (Comment Card). These cards are designed to quickly and easily identify shifts, changes, concerns or successes. Click the Settings icon (*) on each card to choose what data is presented in the card view, such as scores, benchmark categories, trended periods, comment card details, like user details.

The dashboard is comprised of the following tabs:

Analyze Survey

This tab displays customized widgets, or tiles, which are designed to identify areas of success and concern. Click the Settings icon () of each tile to control how data is presented.

View Survey

This tab lists all Model and Custom Questions in an expandable list to view detailed data for each question of the survey. Click the Settings icon () fin the title bar of each group to control which questions are active.

See Respondents

This tab is a searchable list of all open ends. Click a specific response to view respondent data. Responses with a Movie icon () contain a recording of the respondent's experience.
