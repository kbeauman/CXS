About Surveys

The Surveys page offers the following views:

  • All Surveys - A comprehensive display of your CX Survey measures and Feedback projects grouped accordingly.
  • CX Surveys - All of your measures (active and inactive).
  • Feedback Surveys - All of your Feedback projects.

Click the name of a measure or project to view the Analyze Survey page. Each measure or project offers the following metadata and features:

CX Surveys

  • Type - Also known as Channel, is the measure categorization (or attribute).
  • Status - Indicates whether the survey is active or inactive.
  • Created - Date stamp of when development of the measure began.
  • Completes - The total number of respondents for the measure.
  • - Displays the Analyze Survey page comprised of respondent data.
  • - Displays the View Survey page with scores for each question of the measure.
  • - Displays the See Respondents page listing of all open ends for the measure.

Feedback

  • Created - Date stamp of when development of the survey began.
  • 24 Hours - The number of survey submissions for the previous 24 hours (real-time).
  • 7 Days - The number of survey submissions for the previous seven full calendar days, i.e., from yesterday back.
  • Cumulative - The total number of respondents for the survey.
  • - Displays the Analyze Survey page comprised of respondent data.
  • - Displays the View Survey page with scores for each question of the measure.
  • - Displays the See Respondents page listing of all open ends for the measure.
