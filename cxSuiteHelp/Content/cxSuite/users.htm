Users & Roles

This section of Global Settings is for Site Administrators only and is designed to provide control over each user's access and permissions to the various CX Suite applications.

Users

In CX Suite, Users are those who are allowed login access to your organization's CX Suite applications. You can control which applications each user has access to by assigning and editing Roles.

Roles

In CX Suite, a Role is a set of permissions you can assign to individual users. For example, a Role named Executive may contain permission to access all of your CX Suite products and data at the highest level of your organization's hierarchy. Likewise, a Role named Store may be intended for store managers and limit their access to data from a single location. See Create a Role for instructions.

Users & Roles offers the following tabs:

  • Users - A list of personnel with an assigned role giving them access to your organization's CX Suite applications.
  • Unmatched Users - A list of personnel
  • Organizational Users
  • Roles - A list of permission sets and users who are assigned, i.e., have the set of permissions associated to the role.
  • Version History - A list of revisions affecting Users and Roles.
