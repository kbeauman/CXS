Case Management Global Settings

View and edit the templates used for internal emails, customer-facing emails, and other types of responses.

All templates are implemented by ForeSee Support. Contact your ForeSee Professional Services representative to add a template to your Case Management account.
  1. Go to Global Settings > Apps.
  2. In the left navigation pane, click the desired type of template, e.g., Email & Templates. Thumbnail images display to represent each of your Case Management templates.
