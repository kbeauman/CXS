Fulfillment

Home Delivery

Home Delivery measures the customer's satisfaction with their experience of receiving an order at a remote location, such as their home or office.

In-location Pick Up

In-Location Pick Up measures the customer's satisfaction with their experience of at a merchant's location, such as a retail store or distribution center. This measurement includes the purchasers’ experience across multiple locations as well as individual facilities. Clients have full control of organizational hierarchies and attributes, such as location number, transaction, receipt number, register ID, or associate ID.

Model

Home Delivery

The questions of the Fulfillment Home Delivery Model ask a survey respondent to rate delivery, order condition, and product. These questions yield a predictive scoring on the visitor’s likeliness to recommend this touchpoint, recommend products, purchase on the next visit, purchase online, purchase offline, and brand commitment.

In-location Pick Up

The questions of the Fulfillment In-Store PU Model ask a survey respondent to rate convenience, pickup process, associates, order condition, and product. These questions yield a predictive scoring on the visitor’s likeliness to recommend this touchpoint, recommend products, purchase on the next visit, purchase online, purchase offline, use again, recommend store, pickup, and brand commitment.

Deployment Options

Standard deployment is through ForeSee® Intercept (ForeSee® Trigger Code) with optional deployments for Email, Panel Studies (additional cost), IVR (additional cost, reduced survey), and Mobile SDK for mobile devices.
