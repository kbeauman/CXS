Edit a Feedback Survey

  1. Click Surveys in the top ribbon. The Surveys page displays All Surveys by default.
  2. Go to the Feedback Surveys section, which may be located below the CX Surveys section. You may also click Feedback Surveys in the navigation ribbon below the filters ribbon.
  3. Click the Edit icon () of the desired project. The Edit Survey page displays, which is comprised of a list of question types in the left rail and the survey to be edited. See Creating a Feedback Survey for information on adding or editing question and topics.
  4. If you do not wish to revise the survey itself, you can jump to the Collection Methods or Publishing Settings, which are available in the ribbon above the survey, or click Next in the bottom-right corner of the page.
  5. When you have completed your edits, you can go to Publishing Settings, or click Publish to Production to instantly migrate your edits.
