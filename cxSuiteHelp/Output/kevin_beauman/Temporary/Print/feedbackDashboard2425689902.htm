Feedback Dashboard

The Feedback Dashboard lists all your Feedback survey Project names, the date the project was Created, the number of submitted surveys in the previous 24 Hours, 7 Days, and Cumulative total. Each survey also offers the following icons:

  • - Displays the Analyze Survey tab containing interactive summary counts and trend lines of respondent scores.
  • - Displays the View Survey tab with a breakdown of answers per N count.
  • - Displays the See Respondents tab, which is comprised of open ends and Replay recordings.
  • or - Displays the Create/Edit Survey tab where you can make edits to the survey. indicates there are changes to the survey that have not been published.
  • - Duplicates the survey for creating a survey with minor variations.
  • - Deletes the survey and removes access to the collected data. It is recommended to turn the survey off unless you are certain you will not need to reference the data from this survey.
